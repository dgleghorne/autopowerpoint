            IPH 233
Praise to the Lord the Almighty
         Praise to the Lord,
 the Almighty, the King of creation!
       O my soul, praise him,
for He is your health and salvation!
      Come, all who hear;
brothers and sisters, draw near,
 praise Him in glad adoration!
        Praise to the Lord,
above all things so mightily reigning
   keeping us safe at His side
    and so gently sustaining.
      Have you not seen
all you have needed has been
met by His gracious ordaining?
       Praise to the Lord,
who shall prosper our work and
            defend us;
surely His goodness and mercy
      shall daily attend us.
         Ponder anew
   what the Almighty can do
who with his love will befriend us.
      Praise to the Lord-
 O let all that is in me adore
              Him!
 All that has life and breath,
come now with praises before
              Him!
                         CCLI 128675




Let the ‘Amen!’ sound from His
         people again-
gladly we praise and adore him!
